Tomato Salad Recipe

Ingredient List

  • Tomatoes (halved)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Italian seasoning - Basil, Oregano, Parsley (minced)
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional, Fresh baguet bread (sliced)

Directions

Stir together ingredients in serving bowl. Let dish set for at least 30 minutes to allow flavours to marry. Serve alongside grilled baguet slices or alone.

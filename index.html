Tomato Salad

Ingredient List

  • Tomatoes (halved)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Italian seasoning - Basil, Oregano, Parsley (minced)
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

Stir together ingredients in serving bowl. Enjoy fresh or allow dish set to allow flavours to marry.
